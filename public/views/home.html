Rules and Regulations

Awards

Competitors in the 5K run and 5K walk receive trophies/medals according to the following schedule: The top three overall finishers, male and female, receive trophies. The top three male and female overall finishers do not receive age group medals in addition to their overall trophy awards (commonly referred to as 'double-dipping'). The top three finishers in each age group, male and female, in both the 5K run and 5K walk receive medals. The age divisions are as follows: Age 14 and under Age 15-19 Age 20-29 Age 30-39 Age 40-49 Age 50-59 Age 60-69 Age 70+
